DRINK GOOD

FROM WOOD

Birch Sap is a refreshing drink from the purest woods in the Finnish Archipelago.

Sip the Birch Sap, to refresh your life! With all-natural and low-calorie sweetener, you can drink it so much with no worry. Drink it after a workout or sports, feel the difference inside and outside.

Product of Nature
The Birch tree is planted in Finland which is known as the cleanest ground water in the world and resulting a high quality of plants and trees.
Health Benefit
It contains antioxidants, electrolytes, trace minerals, xylytol, fructose and vitamin c which can naturally detox your body.
Super Drink
The purity of the water by adding no preservatives that makes every drop of water is worth a sip.
Naturally Rare
The Birch Sap could only be harvested in traditional ways for a few weeks between winter and spring time in its finest condition.
Our Can - LamiCan
LamiCan is 70% paperboard which is made from wood fibre—A renewable natural product, fully recyclable.
The cans keep the water cold for a longer time and maintain it to be fresh and cool.
Aseptic package solution –Less additives needed, retains vitamins, nutritional values, taste and appearance.
No cooling is needed for storage, transportations or for placement in store—Less carbon footprint.
